Boys gas calorimeter (gas calorimeter) (2019)

The calorific value of gaseous fuels is determined by burning a known volume of gas sample in a combustion chamber. The released heat is quantitatively absorbed by cooling water, circulated through the copper coils surrounded the combustion chamber. The mass of the cooling water and its rise in temperature are noted. The mass of water produced by condensation of steam is also recorded. The calorific value of the gas sample is then calculated from these data

STATISTICAL ANALYSIS OF FOUNDRY SAND (01/2022 - Present)

The STATISTICAL ANALYSIS OF FOUNDRY SAND is a process of finding defects in a foundry sand properties. A casting defect is an undesired irregularity in a metal casting process. Some defects can be tolerated while others can be repaired, otherwise they must be eliminated. A properly designed casting, a properly prepared mould and properly melted metal should result in a sound casting. However, if proper control is not exercised a variety of defects may occur
